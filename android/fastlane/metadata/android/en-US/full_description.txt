Hafiz is a non-profit Quran memorization (Hifz) app designed to help Muslims memorize and practice the Holy Quran with ease.

<b>Key Features:</b>

📖 <b>Complete Quran</b> - Read all 114 Surahs with authentic Uthmani script

🎙️ <b>Voice Verification</b> - Verify your recitation with on-device speech recognition

🔖 <b>Bookmarks</b> - Save your reading position and important verses

📝 <b>Practice List</b> - Mark verses you find difficult for focused practice

👁️ <b>Hifz Mode</b> - Blur verses to test your memorization

🔍 <b>Search</b> - Find verses quickly in Arabic or English

📑 <b>Juz (Para) Index</b> - Navigate easily by the 30 Juz sections

🌙 <b>Dark Mode</b> - Comfortable reading day or night

🌐 <b>Multilingual</b> - English and Arabic support

📱 <b>Works Offline</b> - No internet needed to read the Quran

<b>About:</b>
Hafiz is developed as a good deed (Sadaqah Jariyah). The Quran text is sourced from verified Tanzil.net Uthmani script for accuracy.

May Allah accept this effort and make it beneficial for all Muslims.
