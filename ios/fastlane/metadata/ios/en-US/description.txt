Hafiz is a non-profit Quran memorization (Hifz) app designed to help Muslims memorize and practice the Holy Quran with ease.

Key Features:

📖 Complete Quran - Read all 114 Surahs with authentic Uthmani script

🎙️ Voice Verification - Verify your recitation with on-device speech recognition

🔖 Bookmarks - Save your reading position and important verses

📝 Practice List - Mark verses you find difficult for focused practice

👁️ Hifz Mode - Blur verses to test your memorization

🔍 Search - Find verses quickly in Arabic or English

📑 Juz (Para) Index - Navigate easily by the 30 Juz sections

🌙 Dark Mode - Comfortable reading day or night

🌐 Multilingual - English and Arabic support

📱 Works Offline - No internet needed to read the Quran

About:
Hafiz is developed as a good deed (Sadaqah Jariyah). The Quran text is sourced from verified Tanzil.net Uthmani script for accuracy.

May Allah accept this effort and make it beneficial for all Muslims.
