## 2024-05-17 - [Accessibility] Mapping Semantics to Tooltips for IconButtons
**Learning:** Localized `Semantics` labels on `IconButton` widgets ensure a good experience for screen reader users, but sighted users who rely on hover or long-press interactions also need visual equivalents.
**Action:** Always map the localized `Semantics` label to the corresponding `tooltip` property on `IconButton` widgets to ensure an inclusive user experience for all interaction modalities.
